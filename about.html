About Us

202space was born out of a love for the art of photography and a dedication to capturing every precious moment authentically. Photography is seen as more than just an image, but also about capturing emotions, stories, and priceless details.

Every client has a unique story that deserves to be portrayed with passion. With the support of experienced photographers, professional equipment, and a creative touch, every photograph is guaranteed to have both aesthetic value and personal meaning.

Through 202space, every important moment, such as a wedding, special event, or personal portrait, can be immortalized in a modern and story-filled way. Each piece is a tangible testament to the trust placed in us, as well as our commitment to providing warm and high-quality photography services.
